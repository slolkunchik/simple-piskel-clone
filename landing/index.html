Tools

You can draw on canvas with any tools and choose colors for painting. There are also 4 variants of paint sizes.

Frames for canvases

Frames give you the ability to add/delete a frame. You can reorder a frame via Drag and Drop and duplicate it.

Preview and sign in

Login with Google - good chance to say "Hello" to each other.

With the help of frames you can do animation and watch it on small animation preview window in the top right corner. There are ability to change FPS rate from 1 to 24 per second. Full screen mode is also presented.

User can define units of canvas sizes: 32x32 / 64x64 / 128x128 / 512x512
